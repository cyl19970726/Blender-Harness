# Animation Rubric v01

This rubric applies after rig/deformation acceptance.

The animation gate asks whether motion, timing, camera, and high-coverage frames
work before any source-pass or runtime packaging.

## Required Evidence

- Camera playblast.
- Camera timeline board.
- Top timeline board.
- Side timeline board.
- Asset-only timeline board.
- High-coverage contact sheet.
- Animation audit.
- Profile-specific animation boards from `profiles/asset-profiles.json`.

## Hard Rejects

- Motion is only a path translation, scale zoom, or camera trick.
- The best camera hides failures that top/side/asset-only expose.
- Highest-coverage frames are cheap, unreadable, or off-character.
- Static stills are used as motion evidence.
- Cultural/personality tone fails even if the mechanics move.

## Reviewer Output

The review must cite frames, not only a general impression. It must say whether
source-pass work can start. A pass here does not accept runtime or production.
